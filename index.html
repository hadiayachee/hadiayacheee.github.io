02/03/2022: Meat Using KNIFE


When using a chef's knife to prepare meat such as beef or pork, it's important to start by slicing with the heel of the blade.
While applying gentle downward pressure, pull the knife carefully toward your body, letting the weight of the knife do most of the work. Make sure to use the entire length of the blade.
